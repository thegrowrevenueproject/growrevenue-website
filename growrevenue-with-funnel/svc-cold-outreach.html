FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
Is cold email legal?
Yes, when done correctly. We comply with CAN-SPAM, GDPR, and all relevant regulations. Every email includes proper identification, physical address, and opt-out mechanisms. We target verified business contacts with relevant, personalised messages.
How do you get the email addresses?
Through our proprietary LeadPumper platform — we extract and verify B2B contacts from LinkedIn, Google Maps, and industry directories. Every contact goes through our multi-step enrichment waterfall and verification process.
What kind of open rates do you achieve?
Our campaigns typically achieve 35-46% open rates and 15-22% click-through rates — well above the industry averages of 20% and 3% respectively. This comes from hyper-targeted lists and personalised messaging.
How many leads can I expect per month?
This varies by industry, but our clients typically generate 100-300+ qualified leads per month. One client generated 2,500 leads and $100K+ in scheduled calls in just 2 weeks.
Do you also do LinkedIn outreach?
Yes. We run coordinated LinkedIn campaigns — personalised connection requests, InMail sequences, and content engagement strategies. LinkedIn works particularly well for B2B targeting decision-makers.
Will cold outreach damage my domain reputation?
Not with proper infrastructure. We set up dedicated sending domains, implement DNS authentication (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), warm up domains gradually, and use smart throttling. ColdPumper handles all of this automatically.
How is this different from spam?
Spam is bulk, untargeted, irrelevant messaging. Our outreach is hyper-personalised, sent to verified decision-makers matching your ideal customer profile, with messaging that addresses their specific pain points.
What is LeadPumper?
LeadPumper is our proprietary lead extraction and enrichment platform. It scrapes B2B contact data from LinkedIn, Google Maps, and industry directories, then runs each contact through a 7-step email enrichment waterfall.
Can I use your leads in my own CRM?
Absolutely. We export all enriched leads as CSV files that import directly into any CRM — GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Salesforce, or whatever you use. The data is yours to keep.
How quickly can you launch a campaign?
We can typically have a campaign live within 5-7 business days. This includes list building, domain setup, email copywriting, and sequence configuration. Full capacity within 2-3 weeks.
