FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
What industries do you work with?
We work with businesses across healthcare, construction, real estate, insurance, SaaS, accounting, IT, wellness, media, EduTech, BioTech, and architecture. Our strategies are adaptable to any B2B or high-ticket B2C business that needs predictable lead flow and revenue growth.
How is The Grow Revenue Company different from other agencies?
We don't just run ads or send emails — we build complete revenue engines. Our approach combines behavioural psychology (Cialdini's Principles, AIDA, NLP) with AI-driven advertising, conversion funnels, email automation, and cold outreach, all working together as one integrated system.
What kind of results can I expect?
Results vary by business, but our clients typically see 2-3x return on ad spend within the first 90 days. One partnership client scaled from $200K/month to $2.1M/month in under 90 days. We focus on revenue outcomes, not vanity metrics.
How much do your services cost?
Our pricing depends on the scope of work and the growth goals of your business. We work with businesses investing a minimum of $5K/month in marketing. Book a free strategy call and we'll provide a custom proposal based on your specific situation.
Do you require long-term contracts?
We offer flexible engagement terms. Most clients start with a 90-day partnership to allow proper campaign optimisation, but we don't lock anyone into long-term contracts. Our results speak for themselves — clients stay because the ROI justifies it.
How quickly can I see results?
Paid media campaigns typically start generating leads within the first 1-2 weeks. Full revenue system optimisation — including funnels, email, and automation — usually shows meaningful results within 60-90 days as we test, iterate, and scale.
What platforms do you advertise on?
We run campaigns across Facebook, Instagram, Google, YouTube, LinkedIn, and TikTok. We strategically diversify across platforms so you're never dependent on a single channel, and we shift budgets based on performance data.
Do you have proprietary tools?
Yes. We built ColdPumper, our in-house email campaign engine that runs directly inside Outlook with zero SaaS dependencies. We also built LeadPumper, our lead extraction and enrichment platform that scrapes and verifies B2B contacts from LinkedIn, Google Maps, and industry directories at scale.
What does your onboarding process look like?
After your strategy call, we conduct a deep business audit — analysing your current marketing, funnels, audience data, and competitors. We then build a custom growth roadmap with timelines and KPIs, and begin execution within 1-2 weeks of signing.
Where are you located?
We operate globally from offices in the United States (Dover, Delaware) and the United Kingdom (London, EC1V 2NX). We work with clients worldwide across all time zones.
