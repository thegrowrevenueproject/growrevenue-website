FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
What is marketing automation?
Marketing automation uses software to execute repetitive tasks automatically — email sequences, lead scoring, CRM updates, follow-up reminders, retargeting triggers, and more. Every lead is nurtured consistently without manual effort.
Which CRM do you recommend?
It depends on your business size and needs. We work with GoHighLevel (primary recommendation for SMBs), HubSpot, Salesforce, and custom solutions. We'll assess your needs during your strategy call.
How does lead scoring work?
We assign points based on prospect behaviour — website visits, email opens, link clicks, form submissions. When a lead crosses a threshold, they're automatically routed to your sales team as a hot prospect.
Can you integrate with my existing tools?
Yes. We connect your website, ad platforms, email system, CRM, payment processor, and sales tools into one unified system using native integrations, Zapier, Make.com, and custom APIs.
How long does automation setup take?
A basic system can be live within 1-2 weeks. A comprehensive system with lead scoring, multi-channel orchestration, and advanced workflows typically takes 3-4 weeks to fully implement and test.
Will automation replace my sales team?
No — it empowers them. Automation handles repetitive work (follow-ups, lead qualification, data entry) so your sales team can focus on closing deals with qualified prospects who are ready to buy.
How do you prevent leads from falling through the cracks?
Through automated follow-up sequences triggered by specific events — form submission, no-show, proposal sent. Every lead enters a system that nurtures them until they convert or opt out.
What kind of ROI does automation deliver?
Automation typically reduces customer acquisition cost by 20-40% while increasing conversion rates. The real ROI comes from consistency — every lead gets the same high-quality nurture experience.
Can you automate social media posting?
Yes. We set up scheduled posting, content recycling, and engagement automation. However, we recommend human oversight for community management and real-time engagement.
Do you provide training on the tools?
Yes. We provide full documentation and training sessions so your team can manage day-to-day operations. We also offer ongoing management as part of our partnership packages.
