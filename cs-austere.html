380% Lead Conversion Growth for a Social Media Agency

How we transformed Austere's paid media and CRO strategy to generate 450+ qualified leads in one quarter.

All Case Studies
"We always believed in the power of our services to transform artists' social media presence, but we needed a strategy that could effectively connect us with the right audience and turn interest into action."
Natasha Baron — Founder & CEO, Austere

Challenge

  • Searcher Behavior Misalignment: Thousands of monthly searches for "Instagram growth" weren't translating into meaningful actions on the microsites.
  • Low Conversion Rates: Visitors were not converting into leads or clients despite consistent traffic.
  • Inefficient Ad Spend: Paid campaigns weren't yielding a desirable return on investment.

Detailed Results

Paid Media Metrics

  • Click-through rate improved by 22%
  • Cost per click reduced by 18%
  • Achieved 3.5x ROAS, exceeding expectations

Landing Page Metrics

  • Bounce rate dropped by 28% with dynamic content
  • Time on page increased by 34%
  • 62% of visitors engaged with microsite elements (up from 20%)

Lead Conversion

  • 5 qualified leads daily — 150+ high-quality leads monthly
  • Conversion rate increased by 16% for high-priority search queries
  • Overall conversion rates improved 12% across all campaigns

Solution

Behavior Segmentation

Analysed visitor behavior data to refine audience personas, identifying key motivations and pain points.

Dynamic Content Integration

Aligned keywords in search ads with landing page headlines for a seamless, personalised user experience. Used dynamic content substitution to match visitor intent.

Targeted Paid Media Campaigns

Launched ad campaigns on Google, Facebook, and TikTok with tailored copy and visuals. Focused on high-priority keywords to capture high-intent traffic.

Continuous CRO Testing

Conducted A/B testing for ad creatives, headlines, and CTAs. Monitored campaign metrics in real time to refine strategies.
