How long does it take to see results?
Most clients start seeing qualified replies within 2-3 weeks. We prioritise inbox health over speed, so campaigns perform month after month.
Do you only build the system, or run it too?
We run it. Fully managed — infrastructure, copywriting, list building, daily optimisations. You just take the calls.
Can you work with our existing tools?
Yes. We integrate into HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, GoHighLevel — any CRM. Leads flow automatically into your pipeline.
What reply rates can we expect?
Industry avg: 3-8% reply rate. We typically exceed this with 27-47% open rates and up to 29.9% positive reply rates through quality targeting.
How long does setup take?
7-14 days for domain warmup, infrastructure, list building, and copy. Launch in controlled waves over the next 2-3 weeks.

We NEVER use your primary domain. Dedicated outreach domains, proper SPF/DKIM/DMARC, daily monitoring. Your brand is protected.
Do you replace our existing agency?
No — we focus on outbound (cold email + LinkedIn). We complement your inbound team. Outbound + inbound together fill your pipeline.
What tools do you use?
ColdPumper (our proprietary email engine) + LeadPumper (our lead extraction platform) + LinkedIn Sales Navigator. All managed by us.
What does monitoring involve?
Inbox placement, reply sentiment, bounce rates, domain health, LinkedIn activity — all tracked daily. If something breaks, we catch it early.
What if we don't move forward?
No problem. The analysis call is consultative. If outbound isn't a fit, we'll tell you honestly. You walk away with clarity either way.
