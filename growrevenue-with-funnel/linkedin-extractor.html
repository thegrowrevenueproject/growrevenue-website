Installation & Setup Guide

Download & Unzip

Download the m365-scraper.zip file above and extract it to a folder on your computer. Remember where you saved it — you'll need the folder path in the next step.

Open Chrome Extensions

Open Google Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions in your address bar. Toggle on "Developer mode" in the top-right corner of the page.

Load the Extension

Click "Load unpacked" in the top-left corner. Navigate to the folder where you extracted the zip file and select it. The extension icon will appear in your Chrome toolbar.

Navigate to LinkedIn

Go to linkedin.com and log into your account. Navigate to any profile page or use LinkedIn Search / Sales Navigator to find your target prospects.

Extract Emails

Click the extension icon in your toolbar while on a LinkedIn page. The extractor will scan the current page and run its email enrichment waterfall to find verified email addresses.

Export Your Data

Once extraction is complete, click "Export CSV" to download your enriched contact list. Import directly into your CRM, ColdPumper, or any email outreach tool.
